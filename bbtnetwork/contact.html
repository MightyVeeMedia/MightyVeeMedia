Contact BBT Support




If you need to contact BBT Network support regarding a missed reaction request reward video (within 2 weeks of the request), business inquiry or any other important matter, you may do so using the contact link on this page. Not all emails will be responded to unless the email is of the upmost importance.

When contacting about a reaction request, we require that the following information be provided before we will respond:

•Name that you requested under
•Link of the video requested
•Date that the donation and request were made

Depending on the amount of emails we have received, and other possible factors, a response may take up to a week so please be patient.
