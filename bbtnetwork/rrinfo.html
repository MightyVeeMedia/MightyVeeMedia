HOW DO REQUESTS WORK?


If you've donated and requested a reaction reward for your donation, if the reaction follows the rules and is approved, it will be placed in the queue along with all of the others in the order in which it was requested.

This means that if 14 requests were ahead of your own, yours would be the 15th one to be reacted to when the reacter is available to do so.

This request system is ONLY a reward system and no request is guaranteed due to possible rule violations or other reasons, but we do try to fulfill them all and stay loyal to you, the requester.

Unless to many requests are put in, and the form has to be closed just to catch up, our normal request days are Friday through Tuesdays and we close the form on wednesdays and thursdays to catch up regularly. All requests are usually checked at the end of each business day.

We do not play favorites and NOBODY will have their requests pushed above the others unless a video is hand picked by a requester himself for channel purposes such as story involvement or other channel related reasons.

If you have decided to donate or already have, we thank you for your support and hope we can continue to provide good entertainment for you. Thank you! *lifts beer bottle*
